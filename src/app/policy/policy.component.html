RULES AND REGULATIONS
Discipline is very important in Dance. By being part of REYNODANCE ACADEMY, I commit to following its guidelines.
AGE:
Students will be accepted starting at 2 years old (boys and girls).
Students will be placed and relocated by the instructor into their respective groups according to their age and knowledge of the subject.
BE PUNCTUAL:
Students must arrive on time, properly dressed in uniform, and with their hair styled (arriving late disrupts the class).
It is the parents’ responsibility to drop off and pick up their children on time. After class, teachers or staff are not responsible for the students.
BE CLEAN:
Personal hygiene is essential. Keep your hair styled back, with your forehead and neck clear. The use of deodorant is mandatory. Do not wear jewelry or accessories during classes.
Chewing gum, food, candy, or toys are not allowed in the dance studio. The use of cell phones during class is prohibited.
(Students will be responsible for any damage caused to the academy and must repair it.)
DRESS LIKE A DANCER:
Follow the dress code (uniform) during classes so the instructor can observe your movements and provide appropriate corrections.
Wear street clothes when arriving at or leaving the academy. Dance shoes should never be worn outside, as they can get damaged.
PAYMENTS:
Payments must be made within the first five days of the month. Afterward, a late fee of $10.00 USD will apply per overdue month without exception.
(Students with outstanding balances will not be allowed to attend class.)
It is the parents’ responsibility to pay the full monthly fee, even if the student does not attend. Regular attendance is essential. Attendance will be taken at every class.
Students are allowed a maximum of three absences per month. After the fourth unexcused absence, they will be unenrolled and will need to re-register to rejoin.
COURSE DURATION:
The dance course runs from August through June, including these months, which must be paid in full. The school district calendar will be followed for holidays and vacation days.
EVENTS:
Several events will take place throughout the year:
  • Nutcracker in December
  • Competitions on March 9 and April 6
  • 2025 End-of-Course Recital at the Performing Arts Center (details will be provided later).
  • Scheduled participation in the Dance a Wish parade at Disneyland, California, on July 21, 2025.
MATERIAL:
Everyone's collaboration will help us maintain a clean and safe environment for our students. Each student must provide:
  • 1 package of toilet paper
  • Liquid hand soap
  • Wet wipes
  • Kleenex
  • Lysol
  • 1 yoga block for personal and group use in class
REYNODANCE RESERVES THE RIGHT OF ADMISSION.
